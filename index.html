This is a collection of works done by Georg Rohr, an architect based in Switzerland. These work were conducted while studying, at University and beyond, as single works and in collaboration with close friends. Study-stations include ETH Zurich and TU Munich, while work-stations span across different offices in Switzerland. The work shown here shall represent interests, skills and experiences, focusing on topics of the already built environment, re-use, heritage, authorship and expression of form.
